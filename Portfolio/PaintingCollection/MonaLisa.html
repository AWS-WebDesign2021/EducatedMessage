The Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo da Vinci sometime between 1503 and 1519 in Florence. The fact that no one has ever proven the identity of the woman in the painting and the woman's bewildering smile continues to fascinate people all over the world. Currently, the Mona Lisa is in Paris at the Louvre Museum.
