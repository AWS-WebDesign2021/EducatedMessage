The Starry Night was created by Vincent van Gogh in the year 1889 while in France. Van Gogh painted The Starry Night in a mental hospital and the darker colors show the switch in his work that happened after he relapsed. The striking blue, brown, grey, black and yellow bring attention to this dark painting. Currently, The Starry Night is at The Museum of Modern Art.
