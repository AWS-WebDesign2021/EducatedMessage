There are many places to go in the Pacific Northwest if you want to experience culture. In Washington, you can go to the Tacoma Art Museum or the Tacoma Chinese Reconciliation Park. You can also go to the Museum of Pop Culture in Seattle, which has plenty of unique art from different artists, or the Pacific Bonsai Museum in Federal Way, that is an outdoor showcase of many different bonsai trees.
And these are just a few places in Washington! There are many more out there to choose from if you want to see culture in the Pacific Northwest!
