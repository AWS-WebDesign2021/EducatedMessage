The Pacific Northwest is beautiful, lush and green, which means that there are some incredible spots to experience nature.
These are Point Defiance, which includes a beach, a zoo, a park, many walking trails and much more in Tacoma. Or you can go to Mount Rainier, a beautiful mountain to hike in, camp in or just play in the snow in! The Olympic National Park is another amazing place to go in nature. It is incredibly diverse, and there is a vast amount of ecosystems, including the Olympic mountains, the coastline and even lush forests to explore as well!
