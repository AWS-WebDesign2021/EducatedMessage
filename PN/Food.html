The Pacific Northwest is full of great restaurants to eat at. Some in Tacoma include Rosewood cafe, a small, cozy cafe that is a great lunch spot. Another great place in Tacoma is Farelli’s pizza which sells great pizza, but also many other, delicious types of food. Some great food stops in Seattle include Noi Thai Cuisine and Fogón Cocina Mexicana. These are just a few of the many restaurants throughout the Pacific Northwest to explore! There is so much unique food and incredible restaurants to discover.
